About ATLA Manpower

Bridging borders to bring you the best talent and business support.

Our Story

Established in 2024 in Tashkent

ATLA Manpower is a forward-thinking professional manpower supply and business support company. Though recently established, our team brings decades of combined experience in international recruitment and logistics.

We believe that business growth relies on reliable human capital. That’s why we specialize in connecting ambitious companies in the CIS region and beyond with dedicated, hard-working professionals from across the globe.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the most trusted and efficient catalyst for global workforce mobility and business operations support.

Mission: To empower businesses with correctly matched skills while ensuring fair, transparent, and mutually beneficial relationships for all our recruited workers.
