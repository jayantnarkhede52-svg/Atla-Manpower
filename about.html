About ATLA Manpower

Helping you find the best workers and business help across borders.

Our Story

Started in 2024 in Tashkent

ATLA Manpower is a new company that helps supply workers and supports businesses. Even though we are new, our team has many years of experience in hiring people from other countries and moving goods.

We believe that a business grows when it has good people. That is why we focus on helping companies find hard-working and reliable workers from all over the world.

Our Goals

Vision: We want to be the most trusted team for finding global workers and helping businesses run well.

Mission: We aim to give companies the exact workers they need. At the same time, we make sure our workers are treated fairly and honestly.
