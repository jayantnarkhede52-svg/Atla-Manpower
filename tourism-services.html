Tourism & Business Travel

Unlocking Uzbekistan for international partners and travelers.

Facilitating Global Exchange

Uzbekistan is rapidly becoming a key hub for international business and tourism. ATLA Manpower’s Tourism and Business Travel services are designed to ensure that your entry into this vibrant market is seamless, professional, and productive. We understand that business travel is more than just booking a flight; it's about navigating local customs, securing legal entry, and ensuring that every logistical detail is handled with precision.

For international investors and business partners, we provide a "concierge" level of support. This includes assistance with Business Visas (E-visas), meet-and-greet services at Tashkent International Airport, and secure local transportation. We also offer cultural and business orientation services, helping you understand the local market dynamics and connecting you with the right contacts. By acting as your local bridge, we save you time and help you avoid the common pitfalls of entering a new market.

More Than Just Logistics

Beyond business facilitation, we offer tailored tourism support for those wishing to explore the rich heritage of Uzbekistan. From the historic Silk Road cities of Samarkand and Bukhara to the modern pulse of Tashkent, we can arrange guided tours that provide deep insights into our country's history and future. Our tourism services are characterized by the same level of reliability and care that defines our manpower core business, ensuring a memorable and comfortable experience.

Our Travel Support Capabilities:

  • Efficient Visa Support (Lounge services and E-visa assistance).
  • VIP Airport transfers and secure local transport.
  • Hotel bookings and business meeting coordination.
  • Professional multi-lingual guides (English, Russian, Uzbek).
  • Customized corporate retreat and site visit planning.

At ATLA, we believe that the first impression of Uzbekistan starts with the people who greet you. We take that responsibility seriously. Whether you are coming to Tashkent to sign a major manpower contract, inspect a local facility, or simply experience the warmth of Uzbek hospitality, ATLA Manpower is your trusted local partner, ensuring that every step of your journey is a success.
