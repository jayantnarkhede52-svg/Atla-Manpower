Logistics & Transport

Connecting people and products across every border.

Driving Global Connectivity

As Uzbekistan reinvents itself as a central hub of the modern Silk Road, the logistics and transport sector is experiencing unprecedented growth. ATLA Manpower supports this movement by providing the expert personnel who keep the wheels turning. We understand that in logistics, reliability is the only currency that matters. Whether it's the driver of a heavy-duty truck crossing international borders or a dispatcher coordinating a last-mile delivery fleet, every role is critical to the chain.

Our logistics recruitment is focused on more than just "filling seats." We prioritize safety records, mechanical awareness, and professional conduct. We provide a wide range of drivers (Heavy Vehicle, Light Vehicle, Specialized Fleet) who are vetted for their experience in navigating Central Asian terrains and international routes. Beyond the driver’s seat, we also supply back-office and hub personnel, including fleet coordinators and logistics administration staff, providing a total human capital solution for logistics firms.

Precision in Motion

We work with multinational freight forwarders, local transport companies, and corporate fleet managers to provide flexible staffing that adapts to volume fluctuations. Our "Driver Leasing" model allows you to manage peak demand or project-specific routes without the liability of permanent hires. We ensure all personnel are briefed on your specific fleet technology and delivery protocols, ensuring a seamless integration into your logistics network. Precision, speed, and safety—these are the ATLA pillars that power your transport operations.

  • Light Commercial Vehicle (LCV) and Multi-drop Drivers.
