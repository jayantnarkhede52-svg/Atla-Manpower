Farming Jobs

Helping Farms Grow

Farming is very important in Uzbekistan. ATLA Manpower helps farms by finding great workers who know how to plant and pick crops on time. We know that on a farm, being late can cost money. Our workers are ready to work hard when you need them.

We provide many kinds of farm workers. We have people to pick fruit, work in greenhouses, and run water systems. Our workers know how to work hard all day. We also have people who know how to drive farm machines and use new farming tools.

Good Workers for the Future

As farms get bigger and better, we find the right people to help you. We find workers to sort and pack food and work in large fields. We can also set up places for our workers to sleep and help them travel. We want to help you grow more food.

Farm Jobs We Fill:

  • Workers for planting and picking crops.
  • Tractor and farm machine drivers.
  • People to work in greenhouses.
  • Workers to sort and pack food.
  • Team leaders for the farm.
