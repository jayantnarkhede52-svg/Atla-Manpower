Import & Export

Connecting Uzbekistan's potential with global markets.

Facilitating Global Trade

In an interconnected world, the flow of goods is as vital as the flow of people. ATLA Manpower provides professional Import & Export facilitation services, leveraging our deep knowledge of local regulations and international logistics. As Uzbekistan continues to expand its role in global commerce, we help businesses navigate the complexities of cross-border trade, ensuring that your goods are sourced, cleared, and delivered with maximum efficiency.

Our services are designed to support both importers looking for global quality and exporters seeking to bring Uzbekistan’s unique products to the world. We don't just act as a broker; we provide a comprehensive support system that includes supplier verification, quality control inspections, and documentation management. We understand the nuances of customs procedures and work tirelessly to ensure that your shipments move through the system without unnecessary delays or costs.

Strategic Supply Chain Support

A successful import-export operation relies on a strong supply chain. We offer strategic support in identifying reliable manufacturers and distributors globally, particularly across the CIS region, India, and the Middle East. By combining our manpower expertise with trade facilitation, we provide a unique advantage: we can help you source the goods and also provide the labor to handle the logistics on the ground. This dual approach streamlines your operations and provides a single point of accountability.

Our Core Trade Services:

  • Global sourcing and supplier background checks.
  • Preparation of all export-import documentation.
  • Customs clearance facilitation and advisory.
  • Logistics coordination and freight tracking support.
  • Market entry research for international companies.

At ATLA, we view every shipment as a testament to our commitment to excellence. We take the complexity out of trade, allowing you to scale your business with confidence. Whether you are importing heavy machinery into Tashkent or exporting agricultural products from the Fergana Valley, ATLA Manpower is your strategic gateway to international trade success.
