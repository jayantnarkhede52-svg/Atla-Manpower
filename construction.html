Helping build new places for tomorrow.

Helping Build Uzbekistan

Building new things is what makes Uzbekistan grow fast. ATLA Manpower is proud to help with this. We find workers for big roads, houses, and office buildings. Working on a building site means being safe, working hard, and doing things right. That is why we find people who know how to work safely and follow the rules.

We provide many kinds of building workers. We have people who lay bricks, work with wood, tie steel, and fix electricity. We also send bosses to make sure the work gets done on time and everyone is safe. If you are building a tall tower in Tashkent or a road far away, we have the people you need.

Safe Work First

A safe place to work is the best place to work. All our workers learn about basic safety before they start. We talk to your safety team to make sure everyone follows the rules. Our team also visits the work site to see how our workers are doing. We want to be sure everyone is safe and working hard.

Building Jobs We Fill:

  • General hard workers.
  • Builders, steel workers, and woodworkers.
  • People who join metal and fix pipes.
  • Drivers for big machines like cranes and diggers.
  • Bosses and engineers.

In building, every day is important. When you choose ATLA Manpower, you choose a team that works fast and works well. We are proud to help build new things in Uzbekistan.
