Building a Stronger Uzbekistan

The construction sector is the engine of growth in modern Uzbekistan, and ATLA Manpower is proud to be its primary fuel. We provide end-to-end manpower solutions for civil engineering, residential development, and commercial infrastructure projects. We understand that a construction site is a high-stakes environment where safety, precision, and coordination are non-negotiable. That’s why we source workers who don't just have the skills, but also the safety-first mindset required for complex builds.

Our construction pool includes a specialized wide range of trades: from expert masons and concrete finishers to steel fixers, electricians, and heavy machinery operators. We also provide on-site supervisors who can manage large teams, ensuring that project timelines are met and quality standards are maintained. Whether you are building a high-rise in Tashkent or a road network in the regions, ATLA Manpower delivers the muscle and the expertise to see your vision through to completion.

Safety and Quality First

We believe that a safe construction site is a productive construction site. All our supplied workers undergo basic safety orientation (Induction) before deployment, and we work closely with your health and safety (HSE) officers to ensure compliance with all site-specific rules. Our recruitment team also conducts periodic site visits to monitor worker welfare and performance, ensuring that our manpower is always delivering at its peak potential while following international safety protocols.

Construction Trades We Supply:

  • General Construction Labor (Unskilled & Semi-skilled).
  • Skilled Masons, Steel Fixers, and Carpenters.
  • Certified Welders and Pipefitters.
  • Heavy Equipment Operators (Excavators, Cranes, Forklifts).
  • Site Engineers and Project Supervisors.

In the construction industry, every day counts. Choosing ATLA Manpower means choosing a partner who respects your deadlines and shares your commitment to building excellence.
