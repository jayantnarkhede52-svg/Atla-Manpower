Making Worker Management Easy

Managing many workers can be hard work. ATLA Manpower can take this hard work away from you. We act as the employer. We manage the hiring, paying, and all the legal rules for the workers at your project. This lets your team focus only on getting the job done.

We always treat workers fairly and follow the rules. This makes workers happy and helps them do a better job. Whether you are a big company new to Uzbekistan or a local business with a big project, we make managing workers easy and safe.

Doing Everything Right

Following local labor laws is very important. We know all the rules in Uzbekistan and other places to make sure your project is safe. We handle everything from insurance to taxes. Our clients save money and time, making us a great choice for big building projects and other jobs.
