Labour Contract Services

Transparent, compliant, and efficient workforce management.

Streamlining Your Workforce Management

Managing a large workforce can be a complex administrative challenge. ATLA Manpower’s Labour Contract services are designed to remove this burden from your shoulders. We act as the legal employer and contractor, managing the recruitment, onboarding, payroll, and statutory compliance of the workers stationed at your project or facility. This allows your management team to focus strictly on operational output and project milestones.

Our contracting model is built on transparency and fairness. We ensure that every worker is treated with respect and that all contractual obligations are met promptly. This reduces labor disputes and fosters a more productive work environment. Whether you are a multinational corporation entering the Uzbekistan market or a local firm scaling up for a major tender, our labour contract services provide the flexibility and legal security you need.

Comprehensive Compliance & Outsourcing

In a globalized business environment, compliance with local labor laws is paramount. We stay ahead of every regulation in Uzbekistan, Indian Ocean territories, and CIS regions to ensure your project remains compliant at all times. From insurance coverage to tax documentation, we handle every detail of the labor contract lifecycle. Our clients benefit from reduced overhead costs and minimized legal risks, making us an ideal partner for infrastructure, energy, and government-linked projects.

Why Choose Our Contracting Model?

  • Reduction in administrative and HR overhead costs.
  • Full management of payroll, benefits, and insurance.
  • Enhanced flexibility for short-term and project-based staffing.
  • Detailed reporting on labor hours, costs, and productivity.
  • Strict adherence to health and safety (HSE) standards.
